# FSU Flying High Circus Digital Presence: Competitive Analysis

*Research conducted: December 2025*

---

## Executive Summary

The FSU Flying High Circus has a fragmented digital presence with no centralized video archive. While preservation efforts exist (Oral History Project, university archives), they focus on audio, photos, and documentsâ€”not video. This creates a significant opportunity for a community-driven video archive platform.

---

## 1. Official Website (circus.fsu.edu)

### Current State

| Aspect | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| **Primary Focus** | Current operations, ticket sales, recruitment |
| **Video Content** | None embedded; social links in footer only |
| **Historical Content** | Minimal; About page mentions founding (1947) |
| **Archive/Browse Features** | None |
| **Alumni Engagement** | Not addressed |

### Key Findings

- **Homepage**: Focuses entirely on the circus's return to operations after tornado damage. Director Chad Mathews quote: "After a very long and challenging year without the big top tent, the circus prepares to return to full operations this fall."
- **About Us**: Mentions key milestones (founded 1947, recognition as "best in the world" 1981, CSTV documentary) but no systematic history
- **Performances Page**: Only lists future shows (Spring 2026, Halloween 2026). No way to browse past performances
- **Navigation**: Home, About Us, Join, Performances, Student Awards, Summer Camp, Contact Us

### Gaps Identified

1. No searchable performance archive
2. No video content on site
3. No historical timeline or browse functionality
4. No alumni engagement features
5. No way to discover past performers or acts

## 2. 75th Anniversary Page (circus.fsu.edu/75)

### Current State

The 75th anniversary (2022) spawned several preservation initiatives:

| Initiative | Description | Format |
|------------|-------------|--------|
| FSU Heritage Museum Exhibit | Images and artifacts at Dodd Hall | Physical exhibit |
| Oral History Project | Alumni interviews | Audio (MP3) |
| Museum of Fine Arts Exhibition | "Trust & Transformation at the Circus" | Art exhibit |
| VIRES Magazine Feature | Anniversary coverage | Print/digital |
| Partnership with The Ringling Museum | Collaboration on circus history | Institutional |

### Oral History Project Details

- **Format**: Audio only (MP3 files)
- **Run by**: Volunteer alumni ("we do it purely for the fun of it")
- **Sample content**: Clips like "Ace Carlson - Bonds", "Bob Dawson - Self Confidence"
- **Access**: Some clips on website, more in separate archive
- **Contact**: fsucircusalumni@gmail.com

### Gaps Identified

1. **Audio only** - no video component to capture visual artistry
2. **No performance footage** - stories without seeing the acts
3. **Limited accessibility** - audio format excludes visual learners
4. **No searchability** - can't find specific years, acts, or performers
5. **Volunteer-dependent** - great model but limited scope

- No video content or media archives
- No way to browse historical content by year/act/performer

### Gaps Identified

1. No video or media archive
2. No search or browse functionality
3. No community engagement features (voting, comments)
4. No performer tagging or cross-referencing
5. Static profiles rather than dynamic content

## 4. University Archives

### FSU Digital Repository (repository.lib.fsu.edu)

Digitized materials available:

| Item | Date | Type |
|------|------|------|
| People and Places Magazine article | 1950 | Print |
| 35th Edition Flying High Circus Program | ~1982 | Program |
| Buick Magazine article | April 1952 | Print |
| Big Top Production Program | 1957 | Program |
| Circus Program & Performer List | 1959 | Program |
| Sara Culbreth Cooper Collection | 1947-1950 | Photos/programs |
| 8mm home movie of rehearsals | Mid-1950s | Film |

### FSU ArchivesSpace

- **Alumni Association Flying High Circus Collection**: Programs, photographs, memorabilia, ephemera
- **Jack Haskin Publication Collection**: Founder's documents
- Open to all researchers

### Florida Memory (State Archives)

- **104 items** related to Flying High Circus
- Primarily historical photographs
- Administered by Florida Department of State

### Gaps Identified

1. **Scattered across systems** - no single access point
2. **Primarily print/photo** - very limited video (one 8mm film noted)
3. **Research-focused access** - not designed for casual alumni browsing
4. **No community contribution** - can't add new materials easily
5. **No search by performance/year/act** - general archive organization

## 5. Social Media Presence

### Instagram (@fsuflyinghighcircus)

- **Followers**: 11,000+
- **Posts**: 636+
- **Content**: Current performances, training, events
- **Historical content**: Minimal, gets buried in feed

### YouTube

- No dedicated official channel found
- Some videos exist on general FSU channels
- User-uploaded content scattered across platform

### Facebook

- Active page for current events
- Some historical photos shared
- No organized archive

### Gaps Identified

1. **Not organized as archive** - content gets buried
2. **No searchability** - can't find specific years/acts
3. **Platform-dependent** - content scattered across services
4. **Current-focused** - historical content not prioritized
5. **No metadata** - performers, acts, years not tagged systematically

## Competitive Opportunity

### What Exists
- Oral histories (audio)
- Historical photos in archives
- Current show promotion on social media
- Physical museum exhibits

### What's Missing
- **Centralized video archive** organized by year, act, performer
- **Searchable database** of performances
- **Community contribution** mechanism for alumni videos
- **Voting/recognition** system for outstanding performances
- **Cross-generational connection** through shared content
- **Accessible platform** for casual browsing (not researcher access)

### Our Value Proposition

The Circus Video Archive fills every gap in the current landscape:

| Gap | Our Solution |
|-----|--------------|
| No video archive | YouTube-based centralized platform |
| Scattered content | Organized by year, act, performer |
| No search | Full-text search with filters |
| No community features | Voting, comments, tagging |
| Research-only access | Public, accessible interface |
| No contribution mechanism | Alumni can submit videos |
| Lost institutional knowledge | Preserved and discoverable |
